See if any of the following reasons apply to you:

<ul style="padding-bottom:10px"> <li>You’ve taken your company as far as you can without having to make major improvements and/or a large cash injection. Maybe you don’t want to take out a loan against the business to fund the improvements/expansion.</li> <li>You don’t feel as well as you could and/or should. Has your doctor told you to make some changes?</li> <li>You’re spending way too many hours running things and you aren’t spending quality time with those closest to you. Are you a stranger in your own home?</li> <li>There have been some dreams you’ve put on hold and if you don’t take action soon it will be too late to give them a try. Maybe traveling. Maybe fishing. </li> <li>You have a compelling reason to move to another part of the country. Could be a family member wants you close by.</li> <li>You really would like to cash out and take “time off” before getting into another enterprise. You just need a break…maybe a year or two. </li> <li>Maybe a falling-out with a partner? Could be tension is causing too much grief and you don’t need it.</li> </ul>
